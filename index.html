Key Trends

Overall Volume

1,325 assessments delivered across Jan–Mar 2026. February was the higher volume month at 757, up from 568 in January — a 33% increase month-on-month.

Suitability Consistency

Suitability held steady across all three months, ranging from 86.4% to 86.8% — a narrow 0.4 point band indicating highly consistent screening outcomes period-wide.

Turnaround Performance

Median turnaround was consistent at 1.58–1.60 hrs across all three months — well within same-session benchmarks and stable despite growing volume in March.

Demographic Profile

Cohort is 50.0% male and concentrated in the 30–39 age bracket (38% of all assessments). NSW leads by volume (1,739), followed by VIC (1,371) — together representing 77% of all assessments.
