Key Trends

Overall Volume

6,119 assessments delivered across 6 months. October was the peak month (1,272), with December the lowest (715) as expected seasonally.

Suitability Consistency

Suitability ranged from 91.1% to 94.4% — a narrow 3.3 percentage point band, indicating highly consistent and reliable screening outcomes period-wide.

Turnaround Performance

Median turnaround stayed between 1.2 and 1.7 hours across all months. September achieved the best result; all months reflect same-session processing capability.

Demographic Profile

Cohort is consistently 71–73% male and skewed towards the 20–39 age bracket (72% of all assessments) — reflective of physically demanding heavy-role recruitment pipelines.
